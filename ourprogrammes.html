Secondary School

Our Secondary School Programme place a strong emphasis on mastering the skillsets needed to manage increasingly complex content. We explore topics and concepts in greater details through custom-designed materials that are aligned to the school syllabus. Junior College

Our Junior College Programme focuses on mastering the foundation to ace the 'A' Level examination through carefully-constructed curriculum and critical exam techniques. Every concept taught will be thoroughly explained to allow students to be well prepared with all sorts of exam questions. Besides providing guidance for students, our experienced teachers will craft a unique 1-to-1 lesson structure depending on the student’s capability.
