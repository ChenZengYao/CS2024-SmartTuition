Primary School Programmes

English

Lesson Duration: 1 hr 45 mins

Class size: 8-10 Students

DOWNLOAD BROCHURE

Lower Primary: A strong foundation will help your child to manage the pace of primary school. Your child will acquire skills in speaking, reading and writing to maximise scores in oral, composition and comprehension. Our teachers will guide your child in reading critically and writing fluently to ensure they are confident in the subject.

Upper Primary: PSLE preparation is the final goal and of utmost importance to your child. Besides sharpening existing skills like answering precision and techniques, we will focus on reflective and creative thinking skills. A step by step examination guide that contains key strategies to every component such as grammar, comprehension, composition, oral and situational writing will be taught to the student and reinforced with an examination crash course.

Mathematics

Lesson Duration: 1 hr 45 mins

Class size: 8-10 Students

DOWNLOAD BROCHURE

Lower Primary: Your child will reinforce essential mathematics concepts and arithmetic skills learned in school. Extensive practices with past year papers from Top schools will be given to practice with guidance, as well as fun activities for your child to understand mathematics concepts better. Key heuristics(problem-solving methods) will be trained which will instil confidence, allowing your child to have a headstart in school.

Upper Primary: Your child will develop advanced critical solving skills as well as apply heuristic techniques that are essential to mathematical problem-solving. A step by step examination guide that contains key strategies to break down complex questions and how to efficiently tackle every kind of problem available in the syllabus will be taught. Your child will master more than 200 problem-solving strategies, bridge the connection of prior concepts to achieve a full understanding of the subject, and apply speed and accuracy in exams.

Science

Lesson Duration: 1 hr 45 mins

Class size: 8-10 Students

DOWNLOAD BROCHURE

Primary 3 & 4: Your child will start by learning basic concepts through our unique easy-to-grasps teaching method. Through these methods, they will learn to formulate hypotheses, answer and make meaning out of the information with evidence.

Primary 5 & 6: Science concepts and foundation taught school will be strengthened. Your child will develop critical thinking skills in order to process scientific inquiry. A step by step examination guide that contains key strategies to efficiently tackle every possible scientific question will be taught. Our award-winning teachers will guide students on unique past year paper questions that require specific science-based skills to get a full understanding to ace the PSLE Science exam.
