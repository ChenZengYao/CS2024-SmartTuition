Secondary School Programmes

English

Lesson Duration: 1 hr 45 mins

Class size: 8-10 Students

DOWNLOAD BROCHURE

“There isn’t much to study for English”
However, it plays an important role in the tabulation of L1R5

Our english curriculum is carefully curated in line with MOE exam trends. We place emphasis on weak vocabulary and grammar. This is relevant as sentence structure affects the fluency and flow of your writing, which consists of more than 50% of your English paper. A step by step examination guide, first-class model essays, effective critical thinking models and notes of various techniques put together by ex-teachers from top schools are provided for easy revision. Weekly timed practice from top schools will be given to students to practice that covers comprehension, argumentative/expository essay writings and summary.

Mathematics &
A-Mathematics

Lesson Duration: 1 hr 45 mins

Class size: 8-10 Students

DOWNLOAD BROCHURE

“I just have to practice more for math”
Is practising more really enough?

Our math curriculum is designed for different Math syllabus in each class to suit the specific needs of every school. We focus on building an interest in mathematical concepts and knowledge which are crucial in solving problems. Lack of proficiency in applying concepts is one of the main reasons students did not do well. In Smart Tuition, our teachers teach topics in advanced school’s syllabus to ensure a comprehensive understanding of each topic. Our exam-focused step by step guide will teach students how to spot common mistakes, learn all the shortcuts to answer every type of question, in the fastest and most accurate way possible. Weekly timed practice from top schools will be given to students to identify math problems and solve them within the time limit.

Physics

Lesson Duration: 1 hr 45 mins

Class size: 8-10 Students

DOWNLOAD BROCHURE

Physics, one of the subjects that is the toughest to master. Students either have no interest or are unable to grasp the concepts and fundamentals which are detrimental to their grades. In our physics curriculum, we first ignite the interest of the students through real-life experiments and easy to understand concept videos. Our exam-focused step by step guide will teach students how to identify the relevant concepts involved and use the appropriate strategy to approach each question. Students will be able to grasp concepts, learn to apply and gain confidence in answering every possible type of physics problem available. Weekly timed practice from top schools will be given to students to refresh their understanding and apply critical thinking physics concepts to the questions under a time constraint.

Chemistry

Lesson Duration: 1 hr 45 mins

Class size: 8-10 Students

DOWNLOAD BROCHURE

In chemistry, one of the main problems faced by students is the overwhelming content to memorise. Our chemistry curriculum and teaching materials are developed by our award-winning teachers that are better than top schools around Singapore. We invest our time every year to review examination scripts from top schools to scout for the latest trend and enhance our teaching materials. With 90% of our curriculum spend on higher-order thinking questions, students are trained to apply critical thinking to tackle foreign questions unknown to them. Our exam-focused step by step guide will show students every possible type of chemistry question and answering techniques necessary to ace the subject. Weekly timed practice from top schools will be given to students to refresh their understanding and apply critical thinking chemistry concepts to the questions under a time constraint.
