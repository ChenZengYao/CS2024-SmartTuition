Preschool Programmes

English

Lesson Duration: 1 hr 45 mins

Class size: 8-10 Students

DOWNLOAD BROCHURE

Instil a love and joy for reading and writing through exciting activities. Your child will learn to recognise alphabets, forming into simple words and associate them with sounds. Our English programme focuses on building a strong foundation before transitioning to P1 preparatory programme.

Some of our key activities include:
- Short writing exercise guided by the teachers, allowing students to express themselves creatively.
- Show-n-Tell session where students are able to express their ideas while building confidence

Mathematics

Lesson Duration: 1 hr 45 mins

Class size: 8-10 Students

DOWNLOAD BROCHURE

Nurture and empower your child to become logical thinkers from an early age. Your child will be equipped with the fundamentals of number concepts and arithmetic skills through our specially crafted curriculum which includes creative hands-on activities. Our Math programme focuses on building a strong foundation before transitioning to P1 preparatory programme.
