Head of Preschool Department

Ms Huang Chunjie

A passionate and enthusiatic early childhood professional with more than 22 years of experience, Ms Huang obtain her Master of Science in Early Childhood Education from Wheelock College, a Bachelor of Arts(Childhood and Family Education) from Edith Cowan University and a Diploma in Preschool Education.

Having worked in MOE, as well as in-charge of overseeing the operations when she was in ECDA, she have a wonderful rapport with children and treat every child as if its her own. She believes that every child is unique and is willing to go an extra mile to cater her teaching in order to realise every child's full potential.
