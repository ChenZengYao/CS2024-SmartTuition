About Us

For over 20 years, we have helped to shape the future of students academically and
holistically to achieve beyond classroom success and open them to a world of opportunities.

From preschool to junior college, we believe that education starts from
young and tuition centres are the ones that empower your child to think out of the box.

Head of Preschool Department

Ms Huang Chunjie

A passionate and enthusiatic early childhood professional with more than 22 years of experience, Ms Huang obtain her Master of Science in Early Childhood Education from Wheelock College, a Bachelor of Arts(Childhood and Family Education) from Edith Cowan University and a Diploma in Preschool Education.

Having worked in MOE, as well as in-charge of overseeing the operations when she was in ECDA, she have a wonderful rapport with children and treat every child as if its her own. She believes that every child is unique and is willing to go an extra mile to cater her teaching in order to realise every child's full potential.

Head of Primary School Department

Ms Koh Yen Ru

Ms Koh attained her Master of Education (Curriculum and Teaching) from National Institute of Education Singapore, and has over 14 years of experience teaching in various top schools including Nanyang Primary School.

As a formal specialist in the Curriculum Development Institute of Singapore (CDIS), Ministry of Education, Ms Koh is committed to developing expertise in curriculum and teaching. She never fails to go beyond the call of duty to assist students, and her positive work attitude is deeply appreciated by students and parents alike.
